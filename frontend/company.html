M.A.R.I.N

About Us

We are a team of aspiring IT professionals from BSIT 2A at Bicol University Polangui, united by a shared goal to create meaningful tech solutions for real-world challenges. As part of our capstone project, we developed M.A.R.I.N to simplify inventory management and support small business operations.

From ideation to deployment, our team collaborated on every part of the systemfrom interface design and backend logic to database structuring and system testing. We believe that smart solutions should be accessible, practical, and adaptable.

Our Mission

To develop intelligent, automated systems that help businesses reduce manual workload, eliminate errors, and improve operational efficiency. Our commitment extends to creating sustainable solutions that streamline business processes, enhance productivity, and drive digital transformation across organizations of all sizes. We strive to deliver innovative technologies that make inventory management more accessible, intuitive, and cost-effective for our clients.

Our Vision

To become a trusted provider of reliable inventory and sales management tools that evolve with the needs of modern businesses. We envision a future where businesses of all sizes can leverage our technology to achieve operational excellence, make data-driven decisions, and scale their operations efficiently. Through continuous innovation and customer-focused development, we aim to set new standards in inventory management solutions while maintaining simplicity and user-friendliness at the core of our offerings.
