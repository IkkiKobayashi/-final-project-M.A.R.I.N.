User Guide

Getting Started

Begin by setting up your account and configuring your store settings. Add your products, set up categories, and define your inventory thresholds. The dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of your operations.

Inventory Management

Inventory data is displayed in real-time, with each product tagged by expiration status: Fresh, Near Expiration, or Expired. The Inventory section is read-only and synced with product entries. Quantity adjustments and stock updates are handled via the Products page. Automatic alerts are triggered for low or expiring items.

Activity & Insights

The Dashboard offers at a glance summaries of key operational metrics, including total products, active inventory, and employee distribution. The Activity Log captures and timestamps all system actions such as add, edit, and delete events per store, filtered by user or date, enabling full traceability and monitoring.

Team Management

Employee accounts are created and managed through the Employees page. Roles include Admin, Manager, and Staff, each with specific access levels. Profiles include contact details, department, and role assignments, with optional profile images. All user actions are automatically logged in the activity history.

Frequently Asked Questions

M.A.R.I.N (Management & Analytics Resource Inventory Network) is a comprehensive inventory management system designed to streamline your business operations. It helps you track inventory levels, manage products, analyze sales data, and automate stock updates. With real-time monitoring and automated alerts, you can maintain optimal stock levels and make data-driven decisions.

M.A.R.I.N allows you to assign specific roles to employees, ensuring that each team member has appropriate access to the system. Roles include Admin, who has full control over store settings and data; Manager, who can manage products and inventory; and Staff, who can only view inventory and product details. This ensures secure, controlled access to the system based on each user's responsibilities.

M.A.R.I.N provides an all-in-one solution for managing your store's inventory, employees, and activities. With features like real-time dashboards, automatic expiration alerts, and role-based access control, the platform streamlines store management. Everything from product setup to activity tracking is handled within the system, making it easier to keep your operations running smoothly.

M.A.R.I.N provides real-time inventory tracking, automatically updating product quantities when changes are made. You can set custom alerts for low stock or near-expiry items, ensuring you never run out of critical products. The system also organizes inventory by status (Fresh, Near Expiration, Expired), making it easy to manage product turnover and monitor stock levels at all times.
