Frequently Asked Questions

M.A.R.I.N stands for Management & Automation for Resourceful Inventory Navigation. It is a web-based inventory management and sales tracking system designed to help businesses streamline operations. The platform features tools for sorting inventory, automating stock updates, generating reports, and analyzing sales performance. M.A.R.I.N reduces human error, improves efficiency, and provides business owners with the insights needed to make smarter decisions.

M.A.R.I.N is designed for small to medium-sized business owners, inventory managers, and store supervisors. Whether you manage a physical store, operate an online shop, or oversee stock for multiple branches, M.A.R.I.N adapts to your workflow. The system is intuitive and accessible even for users without technical expertise.

User Guide

Getting Started

To begin, create an account and log in via the M.A.R.I.N dashboard. Once logged in, you can add your products, categorize them, define stock thresholds, and start monitoring your inventory and sales. The dashboard gives you a real-time overview of recent activities and performance indicators.

Inventory Sorting

Organize inventory by category, type, brand, or popularity. M.A.R.I.N automatically prioritizes listings based on stock availability and sales performance, highlighting items needing restocking or promotion.

Sales Tracking

The system logs all sales details and analyzes patterns including top-performing products, seasonal trends, and slow-moving stock to help you make informed restocking and marketing decisions.

Automated Stock Updates

Stock levels update automatically when items are added, sold, or restocked. Configure alerts for low stock, overstock, or inactive products to maintain optimal inventory levels.

Report Generation

Generate detailed reports on inventory movement, sales performance, and product trends. Export reports or view them in real-time for audits, planning, and performance reviews.

Product Organization

Quickly locate products using search filters by name, ID code, or category. Update, delete, or reorganize items efficiently with full control and visibility over your inventory.
